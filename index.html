Food for Funds:2023

FOOD FOR FUNDS

Food for Funds examines how food can be used as a fundraising tactic. Food for Funds specifically examines the Grilled Cheesy with the Dee Zees event held by the Epsilon Omicron chapter at Western Illinois University. In this philanthropy event, the Delta Zeta chapter held a grilled cheese dinner where they raised money for the Starkey Hearing Foundation. Food for Funds examines the research that led to the event, event logistics, and event success.
